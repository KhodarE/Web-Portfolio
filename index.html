DES Encryption and Decryption


DES Encryption and Decryption Program

Data Encryption Standard or also known as DES, is a encryption and decryption algorithm that encrypts plaintext to protect it from being read and inaccessible to unauthorised individuals. Once it has been encrypted it can be sent to the rightful owner and it will be decrypted so the recipient can view the data. DSA is used by some systems and websites, but it has been outdated and there are better forms of encryption and decryption like RSA. However, that's not to say DSA is useless, it is a very effective encryption and decryption algorithm. DSA is used by websites and systems to protect individuals data. As it provides the users of a system the assurance that their private information such as their details, passwords, and credit card information is encapsulated and protected from hackers and the general public from attaining access to the precious data.

RSA Encryption and Decryption
RSA Encryption and Decryption


RSA Encryption and Decryption Program

Rivest–Shamir–Adleman or also known as RSA, is a encryption and decryption that uses asymmetric cryptography algorithm. It uses two different keys to encrypt and decrypt. The first key being the public key that is used to encrypt and the private key is used to decrypt the message to plaintext so it can be viewed by the recipent.

RSA is used in modern systems to protect individuals data on the internet and company systems. Encryption is needed in all systems as it provides the users of a system the assurance that their private information such as their details, passwords, and credit card information is encapsulated and protected from hackers and the general public from attaining access to the precious data.

AVL Tree Dictionary


AVL Tree Dictionary Program

The Dictionary to AVL Tree program is a very sophisticated program as it reads text files, containing words to create an AVL Tree. It selects each word placing them into a leaf node, to check if the word is either going to be placed to the left, right or be selected as the node. It keeps traversing until all the words in the dictionary/text file are inserted into the AVL Tree.
The application also consists of the following functionalities:

  • Load dictionary
